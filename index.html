The Compiler Wall

The boundary where probabilistic intelligence must become deterministic infrastructure.

Every time computing faced a trust crisis -- portability, correctness, reproducibility, safety -- the answer was a better compiler. FORTRAN, C, LLVM, the JVM. LLVM decoupled language front-ends from hardware back-ends through a universal intermediate representation. DMX applies the same architectural pattern to AI: decouple probabilistic understanding from deterministic compilation through a cognitive IR.

How It Works

Four phases. One deterministic pipeline.

Architectural Guarantees

Structural properties that require compilation architecture by design.
