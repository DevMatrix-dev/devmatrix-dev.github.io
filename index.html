DevMatrix is model-agnostic infrastructure designed to sit downstream of reasoning models, turning their outputs into deterministic, verifiable, and auditable systems.

Deterministic compilation, governance, and auditability for AI-generated systems.

What problem this solves

  • Verification, QA, and compliance break at scale
  • DevMatrix compiles governance into the system itself

Where it fits

Reasoning Models ↓ Specifications ↓ DevMatrix ↓ Deterministic Systems
